About Page

This crop yield recommendation tool is designed to assist farmers and gardeners in determining the optimal crops to cultivate based on their local conditions. By inputting key variables such as Nitrogen content, Phosphorus content, Temperature, Humidity, Soil pH, and rainfall, users can receive tailored recommendations to maximize their yield and minimize losses. This tool is beneficial for both small-scale and large-scale agricultural operations, helping to ensure more efficient and profitable farming practices. Created by: Patricia Mae Quitoriano, BSIT, Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP)
